Common Questions About Our IT Support Services

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our IT support services, response times, and support packages.

We handle a wide range of IT issues including hardware problems, software troubleshooting, network issues, security concerns, data recovery, and system maintenance. Our team is certified to work with various operating systems and business applications.

Our Basic Support package offers 8/5 support during business hours. Business Pro and Enterprise clients receive 24/7 support coverage with priority response times for critical issues.

Response times vary by support package and issue severity. Critical issues for Business Pro and Enterprise clients receive immediate attention. Basic Support requests are typically addressed within 4 business hours.
